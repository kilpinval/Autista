# Music Therapy Module: MODULO_MUSICOTERAPIA

## Overview
This module is designed to utilize frequency-based sounds, automatic behavioral anticipation, and sensory regulation features to support autistic children. It aims to prevent anxiety and transform disruptive behaviors through calming music generation.

## Features
- **Frequency-Based Sounds**: Incorporates various sound frequencies that promote relaxation and focus.
- **Automatic Behavioral Anticipation**: Utilizes advanced algorithms to predict behavioral changes and adjust the soundscape accordingly.
- **Sensory Regulation**: Provides sensory stimuli tailored to individual needs, promoting a calm and supportive environment.
- **Calm Music Generation**: Generates soothing music to help ease anxiety and foster a sense of safety and well-being.

## Goals
- Prevent anxiety triggers in autistic children.
- Encourage positive behavior through engaging sensory experiences.
- Provide a tool for therapists and caregivers to effectively manage disruptive behaviors and promote emotional regulation.
